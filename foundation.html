The Foundation

Mafakathini, South Africa
A school for children with disabilities.
No running water. No kitchen. One lightbulb. Teachers walked 10 miles to ask for help.
Two weeks. 25+ volunteers across two countries.
Repainted walls, replaced windows, installed bathroom and kitchen, repaired roof, floors, sidewalk, insulation, lighting, swing set, and garden.
The school served more than twice as many students when finished.
Eagle Scout — 2018. Featured in Scouting Magazine.

Before I was automating workflows, I was learning that the best projects start with someone else's problem.
