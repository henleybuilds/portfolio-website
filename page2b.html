Shelter Bricks for Paws

I volunteer at an animal shelter and wanted to see if I could make the adoption process more visible. I built a workflow that pulls available animals from the shelter database, writes short social posts for each one, and schedules them automatically. The goal was simple: more eyes on the animals, more adoptions.
